Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information to the customers of the Rafting business. This information includes details on rafting tours, tour dates, contact information, etc.

Audience

Target Audience Statement

Our rafting website aims to attract adventure enthusiasts of various ages, including young adults, families, and corporate groups. These individuals are typically interested in outdoor activities and seek memorable experiences.

Scenario Questions

Who are the target customers?

Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts, ranging from young adults to families and corporate groups.

What do they want?

They want a safe, exciting, and memorable rafting experience. They seek detailed information about the rafting tours, including difficulty levels, safety measures, pricing, and booking options.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

They expect to find details about the rafting experiences offered by the company, including tour descriptions, safety protocols, customer reviews, pricing, and availability. They will also look for easy online booking options and contact information.
