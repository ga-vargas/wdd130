Overview

Purpose

The purpose of creating an online presence for the rafting business is to increase visibility, attract potential customers, and show our exciting rafting adventures and services easily and effectively. This will help us grow bookings and establish ourselves as a top choice for rafting experiences in our area.

Audience

Target Audience Statement: Our rafting website aims to attract adventure enthusiasts of various ages, including young adults, families, and corporate groups. These individuals are typically interested in outdoor activities, and seek for memorable experiences. Scenario Questions: Who are the target customers? Our target customers are adventure enthusiasts, ranging from young adults to families and corporate groups. What do they want? They want a safe, exciting, and memorable rafting experience. They seek detailed information about the rafting tours, including difficulty levels, safety measures, pricing, and booking options. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find details about the rafting experiences offered by the company, including tour descriptions, safety protocols, customer reviews, pricing, and availability. They will also look for easy online booking options and contact information.
